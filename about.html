Our story

We are an after-school academic club at Liberty High School founded in 2018 and we meet every Thursday after school. Our club is a collaboration of ideas where team members share their passions & skills: programming, building a chassis, attachments, wiring, engineering, recording, etc.


Our accomplishments

January 2019

Our first tournament was the "GREENHILL SCHOOL TOURNAMENT" in Addison, Texas on January 12th.


February 2019

On February 2nd, 2019 we had our qualifier the "HEDRICK TOURNAMENT IN ROVER ROCKUS SEASON" in Lewisville, Texas. This was a very large event -32 teams- We won the 2ND QUALIFIER FINALIST ALLIANCE TROPHY. alsowe were the 1st runner up for the Motivate award.
